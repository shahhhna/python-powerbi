#  Customer Churn & Revenue Impact Analysis

### End-to-End Data Analytics Project (Python + Power BI)

---

##  Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn behavior and its financial impact using a complete analytics workflow:

* **Data Cleaning & Preprocessing in Python**
* **Interactive Dashboard Development in Power BI**

The objective is to identify churn drivers, quantify revenue loss, and provide actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention and profitability.

##  Key Insights

* Month-to-month contracts have the highest churn.
* Customers within first 6 months are high risk.
* High revenue customers contribute most to revenue loss.
* Fiber optic users show higher churn rate.
* A small retention improvement can significantly increase revenue.

---

##  Business Recommendations

* Promote long-term contracts with incentives.
* Strengthen onboarding for new customers.
* Focus retention campaigns on high-value customers.
* Improve payment experience.
* Implement predictive churn monitoring.
